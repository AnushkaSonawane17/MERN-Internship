Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut

The Koenigsegg Jesko Absolut is a high-performance hypercar designed for unparalleled speed and aerodynamics. It features a massive 1,600 horsepower engine and a remarkable drag coefficient of just 0.278 Cd, making it one of the fastest production cars ever built, with expectations to exceed 500 km/h (310 mph) . The design focuses on minimizing drag by removing the large rear wing found on its track-focused sibling, the Jesko Attack, which significantly reduces downforce . The Jesko Absolut is a testament to Koenigsegg's engineering prowess, aiming to set new records in automotive performance .
